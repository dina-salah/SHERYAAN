LEARN ABOUT BLOOD DONATION




Donors are connected to a machine that separates platelets and some plasma from the blood and returns the red cells (and most of the plasma) back to the donor. Donating platelets takes approximately 90 minutes. You can donate platelets every 7 days, up to 24 times a year. Everyone who is eligible can donate blood, but depending on your blood type, you're only able to receive certain types of blood, plasma or platelets.
